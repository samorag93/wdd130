About Us

At domus seller, we know the real estate market better than anyone. After all, we all want a home of our own! our professional team has been connecting the right people with the right properties, and we want to do the same for you. We participate and stay involved in all stages of the property buying or renting process. Whether you want to buy or rent a property, we promise to work hard to make sure you get the best possible deal. Obviously at first glance everything seems very easy, but one of the frequent questions will be: what requirements do I need to be able to access this home? Or also, how is the financing? These and other frequently asked questions will be answered on the same site and because each client will have their own doubts, they will try to contact those interested by email, by phone or they will be given the option of making an appointment at our offices and attending these or more doubts from our clients.
